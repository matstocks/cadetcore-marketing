Up and Running in Three Steps

No lengthy onboarding. No migration headaches. Your unit can be live within a day.

1

Set Up Your Unit

We configure a fresh install with your ranks, modules, and admin account. All Sea Cadet, Royal Marines, and Junior syllabus modules come pre-loaded.

2

Sync Westminster Data

Upload your Westminster CSV exports. The system matches module codes, creates cadet records, and brings in all existing completions automatically.

3

Plan & Track Training

Use the dashboard to see progress, the session planner to prepare training nights, and class slips to record what's been taught. Everything in one place.
