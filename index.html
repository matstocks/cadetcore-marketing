Your Data Is Protected

Cadet data is sensitive. The platform is built with security at every level — not bolted on as an afterthought.

AES-256 Encryption

All personal data is encrypted at the column level. Even direct database access reveals nothing.

Two-Factor Authentication

Every account is protected by TOTP-based 2FA. Admins control enforcement per user.

Encrypted Backups

Password-protected backups with automatic schema migration. Restore to any compatible version.

Full Audit Trail

Every action is logged. Exportable reports for inspections and compliance.

Granular Permissions

Staff only see the cadets and pages relevant to their role. No over-sharing of data.
