Install as an App

CadetCore can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on any device. Add it to your home screen for quick access — it looks and feels like a native app, no app store required.

iPhone & iPad

Tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen". Opens full-screen without the browser bar.

Android

Chrome shows an "Install App" banner automatically. One tap and it's on your home screen.

Desktop

Click the install icon in the Chrome address bar. Runs as a standalone window.
